About Us

At Room To Spare, we only have room in our hearts for bowling. We decided to share that passion with the world by selling only the best professional grade bowling equipment and products.

We started out as a simple bowling team with the signular mission of taking the bowling world by storm. After spending several years on the bowling circuit, we dicided to "strike" off on our own to focus on helping others embrace the amazing way of life that is bowling.
